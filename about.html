About Me

Your Photo Here

Welcome! I'm a writer, thinker, and researcher passionate about exploring the depths of human experience through various forms of expression. My work spans academic research, creative writing, and philosophical inquiry.

This site serves as a repository for my intellectual pursuits - from rigorous academic essays and thesis work to creative stories and philosophical musings. Each piece represents a step in my ongoing journey of understanding and articulating the complexities of our world.

My academic background includes a bachelors in philosophy and chemical engineering, and I'm currently pursuing a masteers in both philosophy and chemical engineering while working part time as a process engineer. My research interests center around theoretical philosophy, while my creative work explores themes of everything.

When I'm not doing academic or professional work, I enjoy casual creative writing, digital art, weight lifting, RuneScape, etc. I enjoy learning random skills and go through phases all the time.

Feel free to browse my work, engage with my ideas, and reach out if you'd like to discuss any of the topics I explore. I'm always interested in thoughtful conversations about, philosophy, culture, and the human condition.
